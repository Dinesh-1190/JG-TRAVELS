Our Story

A Journey Built on Trust

Founded in 2015 in the heart of Angul, Odisha, Jagannath Travels started with a single vehicle and a simple promise — to provide safe, comfortable, and reliable transportation to the people of our region.

Over the years, what started as a local taxi service grew into one of Odisha's most respected car rental agencies. Today, we operate a fleet of 15+ premium vehicles, serve 1,500+ satisfied customers, and have earned a perfect 5.0 Google rating — a testament to our dedication.

Our Journey

A Decade of Growth

From humble beginnings on the roads of Angul to a premium fleet serving all of Odisha — here's how Jagannath Travels has grown over the years.

2015
The Beginning
Started with 1 Swift Dzire, serving local Angul routes and airport transfers.
2017
Fleet Expansion
Added Toyota Innova and Mahindra Scorpio. Began outstation services across Odisha.
2019
Corporate Tie-Ups
Secured contracts with corporate firms for employee transportation in Angul & Talcher.
2021
Online Booking Launch
Launched digital booking system. Started serving 50+ customers per month.
2024
5-Star Milestone
Achieved 5.0 Google Rating with 161 reviews. Fleet grew to 15+ premium vehicles.
