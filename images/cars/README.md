# Car Images Guide

Please add the following vehicle images to this folder:

## Required Images:

1. **bolero.jpg** - Mahindra Bolero
2. **camper.jpg** - Force Camper
3. **dzire.jpg** - Swift Dzire (Maruti Suzuki)
4. **scorpio.jpg** - Mahindra Scorpio
5. **ertiga.jpg** - Maruti Ertiga
6. **innova.jpg** - Toyota Innova

## Image Specifications:

- **Format**: JPG or JPEG (preferred), PNG also works
- **Recommended Size**: 800x600 pixels or 1200x800 pixels
- **Aspect Ratio**: 4:3 or 16:9 landscape orientation
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB per image for faster loading
- **Quality**: High quality, professional photos showing the vehicle from a front 3/4 angle

## How to Add Images:

1. Download or take photos of each vehicle
2. Rename them exactly as listed above (case-sensitive)
3. Place them in this folder: `/images/cars/`
4. The website will automatically display them

## Where to Find Images:

You can:
- Take photos of your actual fleet vehicles
- Use stock photos from free sites like Unsplash, Pexels
- Search for "[Vehicle Name] official photo" on Google

## Tips:

- Use consistent lighting and background for all photos
- Show the vehicles from similar angles
- Ensure images are clear and high resolution
- Consider using white or light backgrounds for a clean look
